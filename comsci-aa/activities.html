Activities

PSHS-CRC-STEMFEST

The PSHS-CRC STEM Festival is dedicated to celebrating and broadening interest in the fields of Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics (STEM). The festival usually includes competitions showcasing the students' projects, knowledge and achievements in these fields. It's a platform for students to exhibit their creativity, critical thinking, and problem-solving skills.

PSHS-CRC-CLUBS

These activities are in the form of clubs that you wish to join in order to learn various skills of your interest outside of academics: Homemakers Club, Dibuho Club, Glee Club, Performing Arts Club, Red Cross Youth (RCY) Club, Indoor Sports Club, Debate Circle Club. These clubs may help in developing a student's wanted skills.

PSHS-CRC-INTRAMURALS

The PSHS-CRC Intramurals is an important event in the Campus that utilizes the skills that the students have learned from the Alternative Learning Activities into fun competitions as a chance to challenge their skills. Intramurals are not just about competition, they're also a great way to build school spirit, develop teamwork skills, and create lasting memories.
