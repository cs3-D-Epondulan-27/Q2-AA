Facilities

PSHS-CRC-FABLAB

The PSHS-CRC Fabrication Laboratory, also known as the FabLab, is a high-tech workshop providing an environment where students can innovate, design, and create using various tools and equipment. It offers resources such as 3D printers, laser cutters, and other prototyping electronics. It's a great place for students to bring their ideas to life.

PSHS-CRC-LIBRARY

The Philippine Science High School - Caraga Region Campus Library plays a great role in the life of students by serving as the storehouse of knowledge. The importance of a library in a school cannot be over-emphasized. While the role of the school library remains constant, its design, digital platform, strategies and tools could change as technology changes.

PSHS-CRC-ADMIN

The PSHS-CRC Administration Building is the central hub for important administrative tasks that are carried out, such as student enrollment, record keeping, and handling of various student services. It plays a crucial role in the smooth running of the school, ensuring a productive environment.
